Who is Janelle Monáe?


Listen to some Janelle Monáe music

Janelle Monáe Robinson (10 born December 1, 1985) is an American singer, rapper, actress, record producer, and model. Janelle Monáe is signed to Atlantic Records, as well as to her own imprint, the Wondaland Arts Society. Monáe has received eight Grammy Award nominations. Monáe won an MTV Video Music Award and the ASCAP Vanguard Award in 2010. Monáe was also honored with the Billboard Women in Music Rising Star Award in 2015 and the Trailblazer of the Year Award in 2018. In 2012, Monáe became a CoverGirl spokesperson. Boston City Council named October 16, 2013 "Janelle Monáe Day" in the city of Boston, Massachusetts, in recognition of her artistry and social leadership.

Here are three fun facts about Janelle Monáe below:

  • As well as being a talented vocalist, Janelle Monáe is also a talented guitarist
  • Janelle Monáe had a close friendship with Prince while he was alive.
  • Janelle Monáe is also a talented actress with roles in several critically-acclaimed movies which scored nominations and wins at pretigious award shows like the Golden Globes and the Oscars.
